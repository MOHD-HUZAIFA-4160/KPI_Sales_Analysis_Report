## **Project Overview**  
This project showcases a **Power BI dashboard and report** analyzing primary sales performance for various beverage brands. It leverages advanced data analytics and visualization techniques to provide actionable insights into sales trends, growth rates, and long-term projections.  

---

## **Key Features**  
- **Comprehensive Metrics**:  
  - Volume trends, year-over-year growth rates, and 4-year compound annual growth rates (CAGR).  
- **Brand Performance Analysis**:  
  - Detailed insights for each brand (e.g., Coca-Cola, Fanta, Limca).  
- **Key Takeaways**:  
  - Highlights best-performing brands, growth opportunities, and areas needing attention.  
- **Dynamic Visualization**:  
  - Interactive dashboards with advanced filters and drill-through capabilities.  

---

## **Technical Highlights**  
- **ETL Process**:  
  - Extracted, cleaned, and transformed data for accurate analysis.  
- **Data Modeling**:  
  - Efficient schema design for performance optimization.  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**:  
  - Created calculated columns and measures for actionable insights.  
- **Power BI Desktop**:  
  - Designed an intuitive and user-friendly dashboard for stakeholders.  

---

## **Purpose of the Report**  
The report provides a granular view of brand performance, enabling data-driven decisions for:  
- Identifying growth opportunities.  
- Optimizing marketing strategies.  
- Enhancing sales performance across markets.  

---

## **Future Enhancements**  
- Predictive analytics for forecasting brand performance.
